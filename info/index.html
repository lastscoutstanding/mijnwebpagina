The domain Sloux.eu looks very similar to legitimate names because the lowercase letter “l” can look almost identical to an uppercase “I” or even a number “1” depending on the font. This technique is often used in phishing attacks to mislead users into trusting a fake link.

Always double‑check URLs carefully — especially characters that can be confused, such as:
